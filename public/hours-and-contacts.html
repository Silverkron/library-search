Services Offered

Consultation

Opportunity to consult on-site all volumes in the library catalog.

Borrowing

Free loan service for registered citizens, with the possibility of borrowing up to 3 volumes at the same time.

Information

Staff is available to provide assistance with bibliographic research and reading recommendations.
