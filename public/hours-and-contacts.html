Services Offered

Consultation

Opportunity to consult on-site all volumes in the library catalog.

Borrowing

Free loan service for registered citizens, with the possibility of borrowing up to 3 volumes at the same time.

Information

Staff is available to provide assistance with bibliographic research and reading recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does the Aci Sant'Antonio library open?
The library is open to the public Monday to Friday, from 09:00 to 12:30. On Thursday, there is also afternoon opening from 14:30 to 18:00.
Is the library open on Saturday or Sunday?
Currently, the Aci Sant'Antonio Library is closed on Saturday and Sunday. Hours may vary during holidays: we recommend checking the website or contacting the facility directly for updates.
How can I contact the municipal library of Aci Sant'Antonio?
You can contact the library at phone number 095 7010094 or by email at comune-acisantantonio@legalmail.it
Who can I contact for information about events or cultural initiatives?
For information about events, meetings, or cultural activities organized by the library, you can contact the library staff directly at 095 7010094 or write an email to the official address. Alternatively, follow updates on the Municipality of Aci Sant'Antonio website.
How do I know if the library is open today?
To find out if the library is open today, we recommend:
  • Check the updated hours on the official website
  • Contact 095 7010094 during office hours
  • Check the library's Google Maps page, if active
