Our Location

Address

Via Vittorio Emanuele, 101 - 95025 Aci Sant'Antonio (CT)

Description

The Municipal Library is located inside the old Town Hall, a charming historic building situated along Via Vittorio Emanuele, one of the two main streets of Aci Sant'Antonio.

The building is easily recognizable by its traditional architecture and represents an important cultural reference point for the local community.

How to Get Here

From Catania: Take the SS121 heading north towards Acireale, follow the signs for Aci Sant'Antonio and continue to Via Vittorio Emanuele.

By public transport: You can reach Aci Sant'Antonio using AST or FCE bus lines from Catania. The nearest stop is just a few minutes' walk from the library.

Parking: Several parking areas are available near the library for those arriving by car.
